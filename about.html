Skyrim for Homesteaders

Your frequently asked questions.

Who are we?

  • We are homesteaders, just like you!
  • We are active players of Skyrim and are just looking to share the joy of homesteading in the game.
  • Our job is to give you the best experience possible.

What do we provide?

  • Information and resources on most relevant topics within the three categories: Family, Nutrition, and Skills.
  • Family covers housing, spouses/companions, and children.
  • Nutrition covers recipes and ingredients.
  • Skills cover the skill trees including archery, alchemy, enchanting, and smithing.

Where do we cover?

  • In terms of Tamriel, our site currently only covers items addressed in The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim.
  • Due to the nature of the DLC Hearthfire, we also cover the land included as well as topics that come with the expansion.

Why this site?

  • As homesteaders, we struggled with the process of finding good, reliable resources to play in our style.
  • There are no big repositories such as this site with everything needed for homesteading. Even the wiki doesn't cover it all!
  • We hope that we are able to aid you in your quest to seek out this information.

Extra resources:

  • Hearthfire DLC:
    • This DLC adds in the option to build your own home from a plot of land in The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim.
    • There are three land plots located east of Falkreath [Lakeview Manor], north of Morthal [Windstad Manor], and south of Dawnstar [Helharchen Hall].
    • Each house can be customized using a book located on the property.
    • Hearthfire also introduces child adoption, which will be talked about in more detail in the Family Section.
    • View the Hearthfire DLC here.
  • Hearthfire Extended:
    • Although we don't mention it on our site, this nexus mod by Kinaga works hand in hand with the Hearthfire DLC to give it that extra boost.
    • The mod adds a lot to the game including apiary buffs, expanded cellars, brewing, new plantables, gaurds for your land and so much more.
    • The mod is currently available for pc. For more information, visit this link.
