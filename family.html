Our top Skyrim spouse options


Because we only want the best for you

Aela the Huntress


  • Who is Aela?
    • Aela is a nord, werewolf, and thief.
    • She is a member of both The Companions as well as one of the five members of The Circle.
    • She is an archery expert and would definitely be willing to go hunting and cook with you.
    • Aela can be found in the Jorrvaskr mead hall where she owns a bed, in Whiterun.

Marcurio


  • Who is Marcurio?
    • Marcurio is an imperial spellcaster who is very interested in Dwemer culture and technology.
    • He works as a follower and can be found at the Bee and Bard in Riften, where he owns a bed.
    • Marcurio is both fiesty and sassy but he would still be a great husband and loving father.

Quintus Navale


  • Who is Quintus?
    • Quintus is an imperial alchemist who resides in The White Phial alchemy shop in Windhelm.
    • He currently serves as an apprentice to Nurelion.
    • Quintus has travelled from Cyrodiil to Skyrim just for the opportunity to work with Nurelion so of course he will accompany you on your journies.

Senna


  • Who is Senna?
    • Senna is a Breton Priestess of Diabella.
    • She can be found spending her time at the Temple of Diabella in Markarth.
    • By following her religion of enjoying all life has to offer, Senna would make an amazing wife and mother. Just don't get blackout drunk and trash her temple.

Uthgerd the Unbroken


  • Who is Uthgerd?
    • Uthgerd is a nord warrior who enjoys bare knuckle brawling and gambling.
    • She owns a house in Whiterun and can oftentimes be found drinking at The Bannered Mare.
    • Uthgerd had attempted once to join The Companions but accidentily killed her target and was rejected.
    • She is likeable, resilient, strong, and will fight for her family.

Vilkas


  • Who is Vilkas?
    • Vilkas is a nord werewolf.
    • He is a member of both The Companions as well as one of the five members of The Circle.
    • He is a two-handed weaponry expert, serving as the Master of Arms and Shield-brother to The Companions.
    • Vilkas can be found in the Jorrvaskr mead hall where he owns a bed, in Whiterun.

Vorstag


  • Who is Vorstag?
    • Vorstag is a nord warrior and hired mercenary.
    • He owns a bed and can typically be found in The Silverblood Inn.
    • He is highly skilled in heavy armor, one-handed weaponry, blocking, and archery.
    • Despite his strength and rough appearence, Vorstag has a sweet and curious personality which is perfect for adopting children.

Ysolda


  • Who is Ysolda?
    • Ysolda is a nord, bargain hunter, and keen tradeswoman.
    • She lives in her house in Whiterun and can typically be found in The Bannered Mare or browsing the marketplace.
    • She is next in line for multiple shops' ownership as next of kin and is secretly a skooma dealer on the side.
    • Although she dabbles in illegal activity sometimes, she is known to get things done and will help save you money what with all the homesteading you're going to be doing.

Extend your family through legal adoption


Children all over skyrim are suffering at the hands of fate! Save them and introduce a child's laughter into your homes.

Alesan


Alesan is a redguard boy who lost his father to illness. Alesan is the only child found in Dawnstar. He is oftentimes delivering food to the miners and sleeping on the floor of the Windpeak Inn.

Blaise


Blaise is an orphaned Beton child. He can be found working on Katla's farm, just outside of Solitude. Aside from having to work to earn his keep, Blaise is forced to sleep in the stables.

Francois Beaufort


This strong boy is an imperial orphan living in Honorhall Orphanage. His parents brought him there a year ago and never returned. Francois is open to adoption until his parents return.

Hroar


This energetic little nord claims his name was formed from the roar of a lion. He resides in Honorhall Orphanage in Riften. Hroar's parental origins are unknown.

Lucia


Lucia is an orphan who can be found begging on a bench in Whiterun under the Gildergreen Tree. Her mother passed away and when her aunt and uncle gained control of their farm, they kicked Lucia out.

Runa Fair-Shield


Runa is the only girl out of the four children in the Riften Honorhall Orphanage. She is constantly picked on and forced to do all the chores because of it. She is powerful and ready to take on any task given to her if she's adopted.

Samuel


Samuel is an imperial boy whose mother passed away while working at the Honorhall Orphanage in Riften. He has resided there ever since. His length of stay is what made him a leader among the other children.

Sofie


This sweet girl can be found selling flowers in the cold climates of Windhelm. With her parents having been killed in battle fighting for the Stormcloaks, Sofie can be found residing on the ground outside of Niranye's house next to a barrel.
